Frequently Asked Mysteries
Must I be a wizard to learn here? ▼
Not at all! Learn algorithms visually through gameplay — no programming experience needed.
Is my account actually saved to a database? ▼
Yes! When you sign up, your profile is saved to Codewarts' persistent database (localStorage-backed). The green "DB Live" indicator confirms the connection. All game results are saved per session.
How does the Water Jug difficulty system work? ▼
Easy: 2 jugs with small capacities. Medium: 3 cauldrons or larger sizes. Hard: complex multi-jug dark arts challenges — and a brand new problem generates every time you play!
Which AI algorithms will I master? ▼
BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, A* Search, Backtracking, and Minimax — all visualized through interactive enchanted puzzles.
Can I use Codewarts in an academic course? ▼
Absolutely. Codewarts maps to standard AI/CS curriculum. Each puzzle teaches a specific algorithm concept with visual feedback and performance analytics.
